Shares of Sony rose nearly 3 percent at one point on Tuesday as traders cited media reports speculating that the Japanese electronics maker could be a potential acquisition target of Apple, Reuters reports. Helping to feed the speculation was a Saturday report in Barron’s that said Apple, rich with cash, could be contemplating a big acquisition and noted speculation about Adobe, Sony and Disney as potential targets.
