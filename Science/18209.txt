 The Second Opinion column on Tuesday, about new findings that raise questions about mammography, omitted part of the affiliation of Dr. Otis Brawley, who said the medical profession had overstated the benefits of cancer screening. He is chief medical officer of the American Cancer Society — not just “the society.” A version of this correction appears in print on November 4, 2009, on Page A2 of the New York edition with the headline: Corrections.
