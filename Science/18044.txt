 The Remarkable Creatures column on March 23, about sex determination in marine reptiles, referred imprecisely to sea snakes and their behavior. While true sea snakes, or Hydrophiidae, spend their entire lives in water and all bear live young, not all sea snakes do so. A few species return to land to lay eggs.
