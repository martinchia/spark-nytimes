 To the Editor: Re “In the Open at Last, a Secret All Women Share” (Books, Feb. 24): Dr. Abigail Zuger missed the boat when she wrote “male readers may want to go outside and toss a ball around for a while.” Men — and especially young men — should certainly be interested in an event that affects every single woman in their lives. If men and women don’t know about the important issues in the others’ lives, how can we hope to understand each other? Sally Wendkos Olds Port Washington, N.Y.
