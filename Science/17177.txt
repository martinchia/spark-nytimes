 To the Editor: “In Fighting Obesity, Drink Sizes Matter” (Personal Health, Oct. 23) states: “Education is not enough. It must be accompanied by restrictions that curb unhealthy habits.” But if people really want a certain amount to drink, nothing is going to stop them from buying two or three smaller portions. People who buy big drinks are making a conscious decision to do so, and only they can control that.    Tyler Mullooly Mahwah, N.J.
