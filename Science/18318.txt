 An article on Tuesday about Eric Schadt, a scientist who blends mathematics, biology and supercomputers to study diseases, misidentified the company he works for and misstated, in one instance, his title. He works for Pacific Biosciences, not Pacific Biosystems. And he is the chief scientific officer, not the chief technology officer. And the article misspelled the given name of a geneticist who has been a longtime colleague of Dr. Schadt’s. He is Stephen Friend, not Steven.
