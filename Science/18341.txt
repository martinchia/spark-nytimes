Advertisement JULY 15, 2008
 To the Editor: Re “In Act 2 of Life, Doing Work That Matters” (Personal Health, July 8): Jane Brody reports that seniors, rather than retiring, should “do something meaningful and make a difference for others.” Imagine everyone did that? Imagine we had an economy committed to just that — what some call actualization and others call well-being or just plain old happiness, rather than to endless consumption? I wager we’d have a healthier population, a healthier planet and even a healthier economy. Tom PrincenAnn Arbor, Mich. Science Times welcomes letters from readers.
