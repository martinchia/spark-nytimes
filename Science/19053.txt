      By HEATHER MURPHY

SEPT. 9, 2017
 
Corey Payne did not see that the glasses he purchased, and gave to his fiance, were recalled by Amazon because he purchased them through his aunt's account according to Steve Tappler, his lawyer. The glasses were an Amazon best-seller, said Mr. Tappler, but he is not yet clear who manufactured them. It was not American Paper Optics, as some incorrectly reported previously.
