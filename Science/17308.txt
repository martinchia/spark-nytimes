 To the Editor: Re “A Redoubt of Learning Holds Firm” (Sept. 4): Sir Paul Nurse is correct in saying that science has no time for political games; however, politics will not disappear. A solution to what’s perceived as the problem of British science will involve politics and science coming to terms with each other. As it stands now, science and politics are both too busy guarding their doors.   Josepher Li Cambridge, Mass. Science Times welcomes letters from readers.
