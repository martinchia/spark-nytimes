 To the Editor: Re “A Pink-Ribbon Race, Years Long” (Jan. 18): I can identify with Suzanne Hebert and other women with metastatic breast cancer. At “advanced prostate cancer” support-group meetings, I was the only one with metastatic disease — it was something the other participants hoped to avoid. I felt like an outsider who had somehow failed. I’d like nothing more than being part of a support group for metastatic prostate cancer. Believe me, we need the support.    Mark Glowatz Locust Valley, N.Y. Science Times welcomes letters from readers. Those submitted for publication must include the writer’s name, address and telephone number.
