 The Personal Health column on Tuesday, about new treatments for multiple myeloma, included an outdated reference to the insurance coverage of a patient, Janet Battaile. After she was interviewed for the column, but before the section went to press, Ms. Battaile learned that her insurer, United Healthcare, would cover her participation in a trial of the experimental drug pomalidomide; she is no longer “battling her insurance company.” A version of this correction appears in print on April 17, 2010, on Page A2 of the New York edition with the headline: Corrections.
