 The carrot and the nuts contribute great texture and substance to this smoothie, and the coconut ice cubes add great flavor. 6 hazelnuts 1 cup diced ripe papaya (6 ounces/ 180 grams) 1 small carrot, peeled and chopped (1 1/2 to 2 ounces / 45 to 60 grams) 2/3 cup freshly squeezed orange juice 1 tablespoon pistachios 2 low-fat coconut milk ice cubes (3 tablespoons low-fat coconut milk) 1 tablespoon lime juice 1 teaspoon honey or agave nectar 1 slice ginger, peeled and coarsely chopped 1. Place the hazelnuts in a 300-degree oven and toast for 15 to 20 minutes. Remove from the heat, wrap in a towel and rub the towel against your counter to rub off the skins. Advertisement 2. Place all of the ingredients in a blender and blend at high speed for a full minute. Pour into a glass and enjoy. Yield: 1 serving. Advance preparation: This is best when drunk right away.
