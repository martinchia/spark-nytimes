Advertisement  Eight Islanders will play out the option years on their contracts, the team announced yesterday. They are UWE KRUPP, GLENN HEALY, DEREK KING, DAVID CHYZOWSKI, DEAN EWEN, GARY NYLUND, DENNIS VASKE and TRAVIS GREEN. The team's veterans finished their physical examinations and testing yesterday and will take the ice this morning in Lansingburgh, N.Y., for their first day of training camp.
