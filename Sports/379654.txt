 Flavia Pennetta routed Agnieszka Radwanska, 6-2, 6-1, in the BNP Paribas Open in Indian Wells, Calif., for the biggest title of her career. As the 20th seed, Pennetta, 32, of Italy, became the lowest seed to win the title. Radwanska, of Poland, seeded second, was bothered with a left knee injury during the final.
