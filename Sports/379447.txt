Advertisement  AP Hornets forward LARRY JOHNSON, the National Basketball Association's No. 1 draft pick in 1991 and its rookie of the year last season, underwent arthroscopic surgery on his right knee yesterday in Charlotte, N.C. A small piece of torn cartilage was removed without difficulty, Dr. GLENN PERRY said, adding: "He starts his rehabilitation regimen at the beginning of next week."
