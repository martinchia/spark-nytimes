Advertisement  DAN HENNING was named the Detroit Lions' offensive coordinator yesterday, one month after he was dismissed as head coach of the San Diego Chargers. Henning, 49 years old, was dismissed Dec. 23 after three losing seasons in San Diego. He replaces DAVE LEVY, who was promoted to assistant head coach of the Lions.
