Advertisement  Miami Dolphin Coach DON SHULA wed MARY ANNE STEPHENS on Friday night in a small, private ceremony at St. Joseph's Catholic Church in Miami Beach. Dorothy Shula, Shula's wife of 32 years, died in 1991. Shula, 63, met Stephens, 48, a year later at a New Year's Day party at the golfer RAYMOND FLOYD's house.
