Advertisement  AP The Atlantic Coast Conference voted today to invite Florida State to join the eight-team league. Officials said the details of membership would be communicated to Florida State over the next few days. No deadline has been set for a decision by Florida State, which is an independent. The current members of the A.C.C. are Maryland, Virginia, North Carolina, Duke, Wake Forest, North Carolina State, Clemson and Georgia Tech. Roger Sayers, the president of the University of Alabama, said tonight night that the presidents of the Southeastern Conference had reached a consensus not to give Florida State consideration for membership.
