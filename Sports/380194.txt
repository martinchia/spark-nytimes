 Viktor Tikhonov scored twice and Alex Ovechkin added a goal and two assists as Russia routed the United States, 6-1, for its third straight victory at the ice hockey world championship in Minsk, Belarus. The Americans outshot Russia, 40-23, but the 19-year-old rookie goalie Andrei Vasilevski made 39 saves.
