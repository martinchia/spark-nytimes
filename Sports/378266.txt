Advertisement  KEVIN WILLIS of the Atlanta Hawks was fined $3,500 and ISIAH THOMAS of the Detroit Pistons was fined $3,000 by the N.B.A. for an incident during a game last Wednesday night. Thomas was fouled by Willis while driving the lane with 1 minute 59 seconds left and the score tied, 90-90, in the game eventually won by Detroit, 100-91.
