Advertisement  Baseball BALTIMORE (AL) - Signed Greg Walker, first baseman, to a minor league contract with Rochester of the International League. BOSTON (AL) - Traded Lee Smith, pitcher, to the St. Louis Cardinals for Tom Brunansky, outfielder. CINCINNATI (NL) - Called up Scott Scudder, pitcher, from Nashville of the American Association. Football LOS ANGELES RAIDERS (AFC) -Signed Riki Ellison, linebacker. MINNESOTA (NFC) - Signed Jim Gallery, place-kicker, and John Randle, defensive end. NEW ORLEANS (NFC) - Signed Aaron Sanders, Mark Ledbetter and Steven DeShazer, linebackers; Richard Davis, nose tackle; Kenny Whitney, offensive guard; Bobby King and Daniel Limatta, offensive tackles, and Derek Washington, Jerome McIntosh, Michael Ford, Randy Fisher and Robert Brady, wide receivers.
