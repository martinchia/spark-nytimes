Advertisement  Russian YURI ARVACHAKOV made boxing history yesterday when he became the first fighter from the former Soviet Union to win a world professional title. He knocked out the Thai champion, MUANGCHAI KITTIKASEM, in the eighth round of their World Boxing Council flyweight title bout in Tokyo.
