Advertisement  Philadelphia Phillies center fielder LENNY DYKSTRA pleaded not guilty yesterday to a charge of drunken driving stemming from a May 6 auto accident that sidelined him for two months. Dykstra stood mute at his arraignment in a Media, Pa., courtroom as a plea of not guilty was entered in his behalf.
