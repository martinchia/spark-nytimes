Advertisement  Mike McGee, a Phoenix Suns guard, Charles Barkley, the Philadelphia 76ers' star forward, and Charles Oakley of the Knicks were among seven players fined a total of $10,500 yesterday for fights in three different National Basketball Association games. McGee, who recently signed with Phoenix, was fined $3,000 for hitting Purvis Short of the Nets in the back of the head in a March 30 game at Byrne Meadowlands Arena in which 10 technical fouls were called and three ejections were handed out, including one to Bill Fitch, the Nets' coach. Barkley was penalized $1,500 for shoving Patrick Ewing to the floor in the first quarter of the Knicks-76ers game Friday night at Madison Square Garden. Oakley, who was on the bench in street clothes, was fined an equal amount for leaving the bench area and Ewing was fined $500 for escalating the fight.
