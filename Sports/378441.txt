Advertisement JULY 20, 2014
 Lydia Ko, 17, broke free from a late tie with So Yeon Ryu, hitting a wedge to 4 feet for birdie on the 72nd hole on Sunday to win the Marathon Classic in Sylvania, Ohio. She became the youngest player to top $1 million in career earnings on the L.P.G.A. Tour. Ryu had poured in a big-breaking, 25-foot birdie putt on the 17th to pull even.
