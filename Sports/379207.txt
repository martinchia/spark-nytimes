Advertisement  The world's top-ranked bicycle racer, GIANNI BUGNO, of Italy and the top-ranked team, Gatorade-Chateau d'Ax, also of Italy, entered the Tour Du Pont, yesterday. America's major race, the 1,000-mile Tour will be run May 7-17 through Delaware, Maryland, Pennsylvania, Virginia and Washington, D.C. Bugno won the world championship last year and placed second in 1990 in the Tour de France. The Gatorade team includes LAURENT FIGNON of France and Belgium's leading rider, DIRK DE WOLF. Fignon won the Tour de France in 1983 and 1984, and in 1989 he was beaten on the last day by eight seconds by GREG LeMOND. LeMond will ride in the Tour Du Pont for the French team Z.
