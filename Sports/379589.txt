Advertisement  Rod Scurry, a former relief pitcher with the Pittsburgh Pirates, the Yankees and the Seattle Mariners, was given a one-year suspended sentence on a cocaine possession charge in Reno on Thursday after he agreed to undergo drug counseling. District Judge Jerry Whitehead also ordered Scurry to submit to periodic checks for drug use during an 18-month probationary period.
