Advertisement  Boris Becker has dropped out of the Olympic tournament because of a foot injury, but will attend the Seoul Games as a guest of the West German Olympic team, Becker's manager, Ion Tiriac, said yesterday in Frankfurt. The German Tennis Federation said Thursday that Becker was leaving for Seoul next week and was expected to compete. But Becker's doctor in West Germany has ordered a six-week break from tennis, according to Tiriac. (AP) We are continually improving the quality of our text archives.
