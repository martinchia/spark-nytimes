Advertisement  A year after he took Maryland to its first winning season since 1985, scoring a stirring upset over highly ranked Virginia and getting a three-year contract as a reward, Coach JOE KRIVAK resigned under pressure yesterday, the victim of his inability to live up to his own expectations. The Terrapins finished 2-9 this year, giving Krivak a 20-34-2 record in his five seasons at Maryland. (AP) We are continually improving the quality of our text archives.
