Advertisement  AP Salt Lake City was among six cities officially listed as candidates to be host to the 1998 Winter Olympic Games as the International Olympic Committee closed bidding today. The other five seeking the Games were Aosta, Italy; Jaca, Spain; Nagano, Japan; Oestersund, Sweden, and Sochi, U.S.S.R. Documents supporting the applications must be presented by Nov. 1, the I.O.C. said. The selection of the host city will be made at the June 1991 meeting of the I.O.C.
