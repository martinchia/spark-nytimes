Advertisement  Mario Lemieux, the Pittsburgh Penguins' star center, who missed six weeks of the past National Hockey League season while he sought therapy for his back, is scheduled for further treatment in Los Angeles this month. Friends said that Lemieux's back has been bothering him again, and last Friday, he pulled out of a celebrity golf tournament. Lemieux, 24 years old, had planned since March to travel this summer to California for therapy. He has undergone treatment for a herniated disk and a bone problem in his lower back.
