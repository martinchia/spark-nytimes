Advertisement  Now that Dave Gavitt has left the Big East Conference, which he almost singlehandedly built into one of the nation's basketball powers, to take on the task of rebuilding the Boston Celtics, the conference he left behind needs a replacement. The Big East has called a news conference this morning in Providence, R.I., to announced the name of its new commissioner. And the speculation is that Mike Tranghese, an associate commissioner, will get the job. Tranghese, who has been with the conference for 11 years, has been running the league's day-to-day operations.
