Advertisement  When the Texas Rangers face the University of Texas in an exhibition game on April 2, the scheduled starting pitchers are Nolan Ryan, the major league's career strikeout leader, and his son, Reid Ryan, an 18-year-old freshman for the Longhorns, who won all-district honors as a senior at Alvin High School in Texas. "I hope folks won't jump out there and compare him with his dad right away," said Cliff Gustafson, the coach of Texas.
