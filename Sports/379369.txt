Advertisement Supported by Sports Briefing By Ben Shpigel The Jets continued to revamp their receiving corps by signing Jacoby Ford, a 5-foot-9-inch speedster who also returns punts and kickoffs. Ford, who has been hampered by injuries throughout his four-year career, caught 13 passes for 99 yards last season with the Raiders. He will be reunited with Sanjay Lal, his position coach in Oakland. Aside from Ford, the Jets have signed Eric Decker and released Santonio Holmes, and they are likely to select a receiver in the early rounds of the draft in May.
