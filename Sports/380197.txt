Advertisement By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMAY 10, 2014
 Norway defeated Italy, 3-0, and Germany beat Kazakhstan in a shootout, 2-1, in the teams’ first games at the world championship in Minsk, Belarus. Thomas Oppenheimer scored the decisive goal in the shootout to lift Germany, which outshot Kazakhstan, 47-20. Niklas Roest opened the scoring in the first period for Norway, and goaltender Lars Haugen made 15 saves for the shutout.
