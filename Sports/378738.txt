Advertisement  The Jersey City police yesterday dropped charges against Giants linebacker KANAVIS McGHEE stemming from an argument with a police officer and a motorist. CHARLES GOGAN, a city police officer, agreed to drop simple assault, resisting arrest and disorderly persons charges after McGhee admitted that Gogan had cause to arrest and charge him, Municipal Court Judge JERRAMIAH HEALY said.
