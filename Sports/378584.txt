Advertisement  The Chicago Cubs fired Manager TOM TREBELHORN yesterday. He is the fifth manager to be dismissed since the season was stopped by the players' strike. Trebelhorn, hired at the start of the season, was the Cubs' 10th manager in 10 years. Chicago finished last in the National League Central with a 49-64 record, the second-worst in the league. Trebelhorn was hired in the off-season by the Cubs' former general manager, LARRY HIMES, who was demoted to a scouting job two weeks ago.
