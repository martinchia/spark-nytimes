 Ryan Hunter-Reay coasted to his second straight IndyCar victory at Barber Motorsports Park in Birmingham, Ala., when the rain-shortened Honda Indy Grand Prix of Alabama finished under caution. A full-course caution came out for the final seven minutes of the timed race after the rookie Mikhail Aleshin lost control and left the track.
