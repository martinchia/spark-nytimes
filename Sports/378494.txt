Advertisement  DAWN STALEY, Virginia's versatile point guard, was named the Women's Basketball Coaches Association's Division I player of the year for the second successive season. Staley, a 5-foot-5-inch senior from Philadelphia, is the Cavaliers' career scoring leader. She has led her team to three consecutive Final Four appearances.
