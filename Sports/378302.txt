Advertisement  AP The promoter Don King sent a letter today to representatives of Buster Douglas offering the heavyweight champion $20 million plus 50 percent of the gross revenue in excess of $34 million to fight Evander Holyfield. The World Boxing Association will be holding purse bids Sunday at the Plaza Hotel in New York for a Douglas-Holyfield heavyweight championship fight. Douglas is hoping to fight Holyfield in September, but a suit by Douglas to invalidate King's exclusive right to promote his fights has cast uncertainty over his plans.
