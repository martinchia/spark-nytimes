Advertisement By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 19, 2014
 A friend who rescued Indiana wide receiver Isaac Griffith as he was drowning at a Florida beach said that he just acted on instinct when the pair and two other friends were caught in a rip current. Mitch McCune performed cardiopulmonary resuscitation until emergency units arrived. McCune described Griffith as nonresponsive when he pulled him out. The police said Griffith was taken to Sarasota Memorial Hospital, where the spokeswoman Kim Savage said he was in a medically induced coma Tuesday night. His condition was critical but stable.
