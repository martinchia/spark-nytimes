Advertisement  Commenting on the eve of a news conference called by RICK PITINO, the Kentucky athletic director, C.M. NEWTON, said yesterday that Pitino intends to stay as basketball coach of the Wildcats. "The fact is, I intend for him to honor his contract," Newton said yesterday on his talk show on WLVK-AM radio in Lexington, Ky. "He has indicated to me that is what his intention is." News media reports yesterday speculated that Pitino was about to sign a five-year contract worth $21 million with the Los Angeles Lakers. Pitino had his contract extended last year at Kentucky to 2000. It carried a $1 million bonus if he fulfilled it. (AP) We are continually improving the quality of our text archives.
