 San Francisco 49ers linebacker Aldon Smith, who has recorded 42 sacks in three seasons, pleaded no contest to three felony weapons charges and two misdemeanor counts of driving under the influence. Prosecutors said Smith’s sentence could range from no time in custody to four years and four months in prison. Sentencing is set for July 25. ■ President Obama congratulated the Seattle Seahawks, the Super Bowl champions, for their victories on the field and their contributions to their community. Obama said he appreciated the team’s overcoming long odds. “As a guy who was elected president named Barack Obama, I root for the underdog,” he joked.
