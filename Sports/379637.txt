Advertisement  Jim Dent defeated Gene Littler today to win the 1991 Merrill Lynch senior shootout golf championship here. Dent won the $50,000 first prize purse by scoring a birdie on the final hole, a par 5. Littler finished in second place and George Archer, the leading money winner on the 1991 senior tour, finished third. This was the third annual Merrill senior shootout, the culmination of 13 weekly events on Tuesdays on the PGA Senior Tour. The defending champion, Mike Hill, was eliminated at the first hole.
