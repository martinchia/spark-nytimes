 Kelly McPartland scored three goals, and top-seeded Maryland defeated Northwestern, 9-6, in the semifinals of the N.C.A.A. women’s lacrosse tournament in Towson, Md. The Terrapins will face Syracuse, which beat Virginia by 16-8 in the earlier semifinal, in Sunday’s final.
