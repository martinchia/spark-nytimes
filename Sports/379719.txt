Advertisement  AP Jeff Filkovski threw 15 yards for a touchdown to Kurt Reiser in overtime as Allegheny College beat Lycoming, 21-14, to win the National Collegiate Athletic Association's Division III championship today. Allegheny rallied from a 14-0 deficit to tie the score on Filkovski's 7-yard touchdown pass to Julio Lacaya with 1 minute 38 seconds remaining. The Gators (13-0-1) scored the winning touchdown on the third play of a drive that started at Lycoming's 25-yard line under the Division III tie-breaker system. Lycoming was then given a chance to match the touchdown, but Ed Dougherty's fourth-down pass was intercepted by Tony Bifulco.
