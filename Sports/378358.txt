Advertisement  AP The Los Angeles Kings may play an exhibition game against the Rangers in September at an outdoor arena outside Caesars Palace, Bruce McNall, the Kings' owner, said today. The game would be played on a specially constructed rink outside the hotel-casino. "It's not finalized yet, but I think there's a good chance," McNall said. "It should be exciting for the fans."
