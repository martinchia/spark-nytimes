Advertisement  BILLY McKINNEY, who helped shape the Chicago Bulls and later the Minnesota Timberwolves, was named director of player personnel by the Detroit Pistons yesterday. He essentially replaces JACK McCLOSKEY, who resigned as general manager after 13 years with the Pistons to take the same job with Minnesota.
