Advertisement  The Hall of Fame jockey JORGE VELASQUEZ, injured during a training ride in February, returned to competition yesterday at Calder Race Course in Miami. Velasquez, 47, finished fourth in his only start of the day. He sustained a broken pelvis and hairline fractures in his neck in the Feb. 3 spill at Calder.
