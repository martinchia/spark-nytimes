Advertisement  A. J. Foyt, the 54-year-old driver who bruised his neck and chest Tuesday when he crashed into a wall during a stock-car practice session at Charlotte (N.C.) Motor Speedway, was released from Charlotte Memorial Hospital yesterday and left for his home in Houston. ''It's the first time I've had an accident where I don't remember what happened,'' he said.
