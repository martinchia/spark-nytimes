 Barcelona defender Carles Puyol has decided to leave the club at the end of the season, preferring to take time off after several injury-filled seasons and putting his future in soccer in doubt. Puyol, 35, has been an essential figure in Barcelona’s and Spain’s recent achievements, including helping Spain win the 2010 World Cup and leading Barcelona to three Champions League titles. ■ UEFA, European soccer’s governing body, barred a Belgium player for 10 matches for making the quenelle gesture in a game. Omar Rahou made the gesture, which has been described as an inverted Nazi salute, several times while celebrating scoring a goal against Romania at Antwerp in January, UEFA said.
