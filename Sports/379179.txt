 The American rider Andrew Talansky was the surprise winner of the Critérium du Dauphiné in Courchevel, France, as he held off the two-time Tour de France winner Alberto Contador in a gripping finale on the eighth stage. Contador was 27 seconds behind in the overall standings, with Jurgen Van den Broeck eight seconds further back in third.
