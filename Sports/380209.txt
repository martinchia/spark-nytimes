Advertisement  Baseball SAN FRANCISCO (NL) - Recalled Eric Gunderson and Rafael Novoa, pitchers, and Andres Sanata, infielder, from Shreveport of the Texas League effective at the end of the league's playoffs. Purchased the contract of Steve Decker, catcher, from Shreveport. Basketball SACRAMENTO (NBA) - Signed Travis Mays, guard, to a multiyear contract. Football ATLANTA (NFC) - Signed William Evers, cornerback. Waived Gilbert Renfroe, quarterback. INDIANAPOLIS (AFC) - Activated Randy Dixon, offensive guard; Jeff Herrod, linebacker; and Jon Hand, defensive end, from the reserve list. Placed Eugene Riley, tight end, on injured reserve. Released Gary Baldinger and Sean McNannie, defensive ends. LOS ANGELES (NFC) - Activated Damone Johnson, tight end, from the reserve list. Released Jeff Mickel, tackle. PHOENIX (NFC) - Placed Bob Clasby, defensive tackle, on injured reserve. Hockey HARTFORD (NHL) - Sent Mike Lenarduzzi, goaltender, to Sault Ste. Marie of the Ontario Hockey League; Geoff Sanderson, center, to Swift Current of the Western Hockey League; and Denis Chalifoux, center, to Laval of the Quebec Major Junior Hockey League.
