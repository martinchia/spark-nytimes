Advertisement  The National Basketball Association champion Chicago Bulls signed former Oklahoma State forward BYRON HOUSTON, the 27th player selected in the first round of the N.B.A. draft, to a multiyear contract yesterday. Terms of the pact were not revealed. Houston averaged 20.2 points and 8.7 rebounds at Oklahoma State. The 6-foot-4-inch, 250-pounder was co-Big Eight player of the year with Missouri's ANTHONY PEELER. (AP) We are continually improving the quality of our text archives.
