Advertisement  He was released after the end of last season by the Milwaukee Brewers, perhaps because the Brewers couldn't remember just how valuable a catcher he could be. Now 42-year-old RICK DEMPSEY is getting another chance. He has been invited to spring training by the Baltimore Orioles as a non-roster player. The Orioles do remember. Dempsey, they recall, was the most valuable player when the Orioles beat Philadelphia in the 1983 World Series. (AP) We are continually improving the quality of our text archives.
