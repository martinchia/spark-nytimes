Advertisement By GERALD ESKENAZI Horsefair is supposed to be a celebration of the horse. Accordingly, dozens of booths lined the side of the track: You could buy paintings of horses, T-shirts, boots and saddles, jewelry and books on handicapping. Inside, there was even some old-fashioned dancing to country fiddles. But the musicians didn't realize that as post time approaches, a crowd thins.
