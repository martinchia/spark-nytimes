Advertisement  Two longtime associates of MIKE TYSON have been named co-managers of the former heavyweight champion, who is serving a six-year sentence in an Indiana prison for rape. JOHN HORNE and RORY HOLLOWAY will manage Tyson's fighting career if and when it resumes, according to a fax issued from Horne's Albany home.
