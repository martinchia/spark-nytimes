Advertisement  TERRY TEAGLE, a 10-year veteran of four National Basketball Association teams, yesterday signed with the Italian champion, Benetton Treviso. Teagle played guard and forward for the last two seasons with the Los Angeles Lakers, and was previously with the Houston Rockets, the Detroit Pistons and the Golden State Warriors.
