Advertisement By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSJUNE 24, 2014
 Hope Solo, the United States women’s soccer team goalkeeper, entered a not guilty plea after her domestic violence arrest in suburban Seattle. The authorities said that Solo was intoxicated early Saturday and that she had assaulted her sister and her 17-year-old nephew. Her lawyer, Todd Maybrown, said she was a victim in the altercation.
