Advertisement  He's no Bo, but Los Angeles Rams running back CLEVELAND GARY does know baseball, too. The 25-year-old outfielder, who has been a two-sport pro for three years, signed a minor league contract Monday with the California Angels and has been assigned to their Class A level.
